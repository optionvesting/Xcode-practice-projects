|bid|

DEFINITION: the price at which the market maker will buy a security from you (i.e. the price the market maker is "bid"-ing on the security).

Sometimes the bid price is simply referred to as "the bid." The bid price is always less than the ask price.

If you place a market order to sell a security, you will only get the bid price.

If you use a limit order to sell a security, you can usually sell the security for slightly more than the bid.

The bid is the opposite of the ask, which is the price at which the market maker is willing to sell a security.
