DEFINITION: the type of order used to open a long position. A long position is when you buy a security you expect to gain value, and then sell it later.

To open a long position, use a "buy to open" order. Close a long position with a "sell to close" order.

For short postions, open with a "sell to open" order and close with a "buy to close" order.
