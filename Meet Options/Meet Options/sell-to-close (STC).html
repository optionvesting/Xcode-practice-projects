| sel | | to͞o | | klōs |

DEFINITION: to place an order to close a long position. Long positions are opened by first buying a security, and then selling it (ideally at a higher price) later.

To open a long position, use a "buy to open" order. To close the long position, place a "sell to close " order.

Short positions work in an opposite way. To open a short position, place a "sell to open" order. To close your short position, use a "buy to close" order.
