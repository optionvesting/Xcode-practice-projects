| bī | | to͞o | | klōs |

DEFINITION: to place an order to close a short position. Short positions are opened by first selling a security, and then buying it back later.

To open a short position, use a "sell to open" order. To close the short position, place a "buy to close " order.

Long positions work in an opposite way. To open a long position, place a "buy to open" order. To close your long position, use a "sell to close" order.
