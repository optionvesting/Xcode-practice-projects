DEFINITION: information showing the last, bid, and ask prices for a given stock. Stock quotes can be retrieved freely over the web via any search engine and many finance sites by simply searching for the stock's ticker symbol. Some free websites and most brokerage websites offer real-time quotes, while many free websites provide delayed quotes that are 20 minutes old.

The bid and ask prices show the price at which you can currently sell (bid) or buy (ask) the stock. The last price shows the price of the last transaction executed by the market maker, regardless of whether it was a buy or sell.

Stock quotes typically provide an abundance of other information beyond prices. This information includes: EPS, Market Cap, P/E Ratio, Projected Earnings, Dividend Yield, news relating to the stock, and a stock chart.
